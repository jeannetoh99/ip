Here are some magic words to get you going:
To add a todo, say 'todo <task description>'.
To add a deadline, say 'deadline <task description> /by <yyyy-mm-dd>'.
To add an event, say 'event <event description> /at <event location>'
To view your tasks, say 'list'.
To check off a task, say 'done <task number>'.
To leave, say 'bye'.
